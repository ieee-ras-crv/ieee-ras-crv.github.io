The IROS 2023 Workshop on Robotic Perception and Mapping: Frontier Vision & Learning Techniques was held on Oct 5 with 388 registrants. Video recordings will be posted on YouTube and added to the [workshop website](https://sites.google.com/view/ropem/). Sponsored by our TC, selected best papers received $100 monetary prizes.
